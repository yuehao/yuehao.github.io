## Course Overview

The [U.S. Particle Accelerator School (USPAS)](<https://uspas.fnal.gov>) is the nation's graduate program in accelerator science, run as a sequence of two-week intensive sessions hosted twice a year on /or near rotating U.S. university campuses. The "Accelerator Physics" course aims at first- or second-year graduate students who want a rigorous, modern treatment of the beam physics in a compressed format.

## Course Overview

PHY 905 is the core graduate accelerator physics offering at Michigan State University. The course develops the formalism needed to describe charged-particle beams in modern accelerators from first principles, then applies that formalism to the design and analysis of circular and linear machines. 

[The 2025 session of accelerator physics](https://people.frib.msu.edu/~lund/msu/phy905_2025/) is listed as P905 Section 6. The course is co-taught by Prof. Lund and Prof Hao.
